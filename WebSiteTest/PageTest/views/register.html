What is SoundCloud
SoundCloud allows you to upload and share your sounds from anywhere to almost everywhere on the internet. Sign up for a free SoundCloud account and join the world's largest community of music and audio creators!

What does connect mean
With access, the app can upload and download tracks, make comments, favorite tracks, and more. Your email address will be kept private and you can disconnect the app in your SoundCloud account settings at any time.
